1. Company Employee Management System
A MySQL relational database simulating a real company structure with Employee, Department and Salary Grade tables.
Includes complex SQL queries using JOINs, subqueries and aggregate functions to analyse employee salaries, job roles and department assignments.
Demonstrates primary key constraints, date-based filtering and multi-condition WHERE clauses on 14 employee records across 4 departments.

2. Sales & Customer Analytics Database
A three-table relational schema covering Salesman, Customer and Orders designed to simulate a real sales business operation. Includes analytical queries to identify top-performing salesmen by commission, filter orders by purchase range, and generate city-wise customer-salesman matching reports using INNER, LEFT and RIGHT JOINs. Built on 6 salesmen, 8 customers and 13 order records with GROUP BY and aggregate-level reporting.

3. Student Records Management System
A simple MySQL database for tracking student marks across subjects with full schema creation and modification history. Covers ALTER TABLE operations to restructure columns without data loss, and applies COUNT, MAX, MIN, SUM and AVG functions for academic performance reporting. Demonstrates foundational DDL and DML skills including table renaming, column dropping and multi-row INSERT operations
